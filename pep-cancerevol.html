Cancer Evolution: what makes cancer hard to treat?

Your dissertation should focus on the following points: What mutational processes occur in cancer?
  • Identify the different types of genetic mutations (single nucleotide variants, chromosomal abnormalities, etc).
  • What is a "mutation signature"?
  • What happens to the genetics of a cancer over time?
  • How does evolution differ amongst individuals?
  • What is intra- and inter-tumour heterogeneity?
What is the functional impact of cancer evolution?
  • What are the documented impacts of transcription and translation within tumours?
  • How does tumour heterogeneity complicate treatment? What do multiple genetic lineages mean for the emergence of drug resistance?
  • What is the effect of "tumour burden" for immunotherapy?
An extensive list of cancer evolution resources is given her: Nature Collection: Cancer Evolution
